About SYD ICT Solutions

A technology partner dedicated to building Somalia’s digital future.

Established in 2018, SYD ICT Solutions was founded with a clear purpose: to design and deliver digital systems that empower universities, institutions and businesses to operate with confidence in a rapidly evolving world.

Company Background

Established in 2018, rooted in Mogadishu and built for impact.

SYD ICT Solutions began as a small team of engineers and designers who saw a clear gap: institutions needed digital systems that truly understood their realities—connectivity challenges, operational complexity and the need for intuitive tools in both Somali and international contexts.

Over the years, we have grown into a multidisciplinary team of software developers, system architects, designers and ICT consultants. Together, we have delivered management systems, automation platforms and digital experiences that support the daily work of administrators, lecturers, students and business leaders across Somalia.

Purpose

To empower organizations through intelligent ICT solutions.

We exist to help institutions and businesses digitize their processes, gain visibility into their operations and unlock new opportunities through data and automation.

Mission

Deliver reliable, user-centered digital systems.

Our mission is to design, build and support ICT solutions that are secure, scalable and intuitive—driving measurable improvements in efficiency and decision-making.

Vision

A digitally empowered Somalia, connected to the world.

We envision a future where universities, organizations and businesses across Somalia operate with modern digital tools, seamlessly connected to regional and global ecosystems.

Philosophy

Technology must respect people, context and purpose.

We believe that the best systems are not only powerful, but also considerate—designed with empathy for the people who will use them every day.

Journey & Milestones

From a small team to a trusted digital partner.

Our growth reflects the trust of our clients and the dedication of our team.

  1. 2018

    SYD ICT Solutions is founded

    A small group of engineers and designers come together in Mogadishu to build tailored digital tools for local institutions.

  2. 2019–2020

    First university management systems deployed

    We launch our early university and college management platforms, digitizing admissions, student records and finance workflows.

  3. 2021–2022

    Expansion into business and inventory systems

    Building on our experience with institutional systems, we introduce inventory, investment and business management platforms for private-sector clients.

  4. 2023–Present

    A growing portfolio of digital solutions

    Today, we continue to refine our platforms, introduce new modules and forge partnerships that support the long-term digital transformation of institutions across Somalia.
